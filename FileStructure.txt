VALAIORP is a GPLv3-licensed operational reasoning platform designed to bring determinism, governance, and real-world execution to enterprise AI systems.

All built-in tools return a standardized ToolResult: { Status: STATUSCODE, Results: {}, Errors: {}, IsSuccess: bool, StepId: string? }
